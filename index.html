Nifty Muslim Sisters:

Nifty Muslim Sisters are created to celebrate the greater contribution of our Muslim women to societies in each country. They are our mothers, sisters, teachers, doctors, nurses, scientists, engineers, head of the countries. Nifty sisters wear hijabs not only as a fashion garment but also as a symbol of modesty, purity, and authenticity.
